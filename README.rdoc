Explore is tool for generating random D&D campaigns.
No need for game master or any preparations.
Simple pleasure of hacking, slashing and looting.

Planned features:
* Random encounters
  - random monsters
  - random places
* Random treasures
  - gold [done]
  - items (magical and common)
* Team management
  - experience and level [done]
  - team gold
  - team items
  - selling and buying items in shop
